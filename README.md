<h2>‚û°Ô∏èOverview</h2>
<p>üü£The Railway Automation System aims to enhance safety and efficiency in railway operations. The system automates the operation of railway crossing bars, ensuring they are lowered or raised at appropriate times. Additionally, it includes an obstacle detection mechanism to prevent collisions and ensure the smooth operation of Trains.</p>
